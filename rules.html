Rules

Dice

You start off the game with 10 coins.
Roll the dice, if the dice sum up to over 6, you win a coin.
If the dice sum up to under 6, you lose a coin.
Roll the dice until you reach 20 coins to win.


Slot

You start off this game with 100 coins.
You have 3 options "Line 1" "Line 3" "Line 5".
If you click on "Line 1" to win, you need the three images in the middle from left to right to be the same.
If you click on "Line 3" you need either one of the three lines from left to right to have the same three images.
If you click on "Line 5" you need any line to have the same three images, from left to right, or either lines diagonally.

Lines


one line plays the middle row

three lines plays all three rows

five lines plays rows and diagonals
